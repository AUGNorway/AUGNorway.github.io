About Azure user group Norway

Azure user group Norway aim to create a platform for everyone to learn and share. Our focus is on Microsoft Azure and we welcome everyone whether your background is an IT Pro, developer, anything in between.

At any given time you can find our next event over on our Meetup.com group.We arrange local, regional and virtual events as often as we can, but at least once per quarter.

If you feel the urge to share. Our call-for-papers is open to everyone, and we encourage unexperienced speakers to use Azure user group Norway as their stepping stone on to the big stage.
